Know Your Rights

Right to Remain Silent

You cannot be forced to confess or self-incriminate.

Right to Legal Representation

You have the right to consult a lawyer.

Right Against Illegal Arrest

Police must provide valid grounds for arrest.

Right to Bail

Bailable offences allow immediate bail.

Right to Medical Examination

You may request medical assistance if injured.

Right to Free Legal Aid

Free legal help is available if you cannot afford a lawyer.
